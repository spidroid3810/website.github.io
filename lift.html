Passenger Lift


Passenger Lifts are designed to suit every construction requirement, with trouble free performance and low maintenance cost. Passenger elevators capacity is related to the available floor space. Generally passenger elevators are available in capacities from 4 to 20 passengers and speed varying between 0.5 Meter/sec to 2 Meter/Sec. Choice of passenger elevator typically depends on type of building (Commercial, Residential, Hotel, Mall, Housing Society etc.,) and parameter such as passenger traffic movement. Reliable Lifts offers all types of passenger elevator whether auto door or manually operated doors with variety of aesthetic choices for interior, flooring and false ceiling. We can also provide grouping of multiple elevators to reduce the waiting time of the passengers. Each passenger lift is fully equipped with the latest safety devices as required.
